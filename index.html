Dividend (लाभांश)

➕
🇬🇧 English

A dividend is a portion of a company's earnings distributed to its shareholders as a reward for their investment. It is usually paid in cash directly per share.

Example: If Company X declares a dividend of ₹5 per share and you own 100 shares, you will receive ₹500 directly in your bank account!
🇮🇳 Hinglish

Simple words me, jab koi company achha profit kamati hai, toh apne profit ka ek chota hissa wo apne shareholders (investors) ke sath share karti hai. Ise hi dividend kehte hain.

Asan Bhasha Me: Agar Company X ne ₹5 per share ka dividend announce kiya aur aapke paas 100 shares hain, toh ₹500 direct aapke bank account me aa jayenge.
🎁

Bonus Shares (बोनस शेयर्स)

➕
🇬🇧 English

Bonus shares are additional free shares given by a company to its existing shareholders. This is done to reward investors. The share price adjusts proportionally, so total investment value remains the same.

Example: If you have 100 shares at ₹200 each and the company announces a 1:1 bonus, you will get 100 extra shares. You now have 200 shares at ₹100 each.
🇮🇳 Hinglish

Bonus shares wo free shares hote hain jo company apne existing shareholders ko unke investment ke badle bina kisi extra cost ke deti hai. Isse aapke shares badh jate hain par stock ka price usi ratio me kam ho jata hai.

Asan Bhasha Me: Agar company 1:1 ka bonus deti hai aur aapke paas ₹200 ke price par 100 shares hain, to bonus ke baad aapke paas 200 shares ho jayenge aur price ₹100 ho jayega. Total value ₹20,000 hi rahegi!
✂️

Stock Split (स्टॉक स्प्लिट)

➕
🇬🇧 English

A stock split is when a company divides its existing high-priced shares into multiple new shares to increase liquidity. This lowers the individual share price, making it highly affordable for retail traders. The face value is reduced.

Example: In a 1:10 split, a share worth ₹1,000 (Face Value ₹10) becomes 10 shares of ₹100 each (Face Value ₹1).
🇮🇳 Hinglish

Stock Split me company apne mehnge shares ko chote-chote tukdo me divide kar deti hai taki market me shares ki liquidity (khareed-bech) badh sake. Isse share sasta ho jata hai aur Face Value bhi divide ho jati hai.

Asan Bhasha Me: Agar 1:10 ka split hota hai, to ₹1,000 ka 1 share (Face Value ₹10) ab ₹100 ke 10 shares me convert ho jayega (Face Value ₹1). Value same rahegi par quantity badh jayegi!

Support & Resistance

➕
🇬🇧 English

Support acts as a price floor where buying interest is strong enough to overcome selling pressure, causing the price to bounce back up. Resistance acts as a price ceiling where selling pressure stops the price from rising further, causing it to drop.

Example: If Nifty consistently bounces up from ₹22,000 and falls back down whenever it touches ₹22,500, then ₹22,000 is the Support and ₹22,500 is the Resistance level.
🇮🇳 Hinglish

Support market me ek floor (zameen) ki tarah hai jahan buyers active ho kar price ko aur girne se bacha lete hain. Resistance ek chat (roof) ki tarah hai jahan sellers active ho kar price ko aur upar jaane se rok dete hain.

Asan Bhasha Me: Agar Nifty baar-baar ₹22,000 se takra kar upar bhagta hai aur ₹22,500 par jate hi girne lagta hai, toh ₹22,000 Support hai aur ₹22,500 Resistance hai.

Candlestick Basics (कैंडलस्टिक चार्ट)

➕
🇬🇧 English

A candlestick represents price movements over a specific time frame. It consists of a "body" (open-to-close range) and "wicks" (high and low prices). Green represents bullish upward movement; red shows bearish downward movement.

Example: A daily candle with a long lower wick shows that sellers tried to push prices down, but buyers stepped in and pushed prices back up before close, showing bullish strength.
🇮🇳 Hinglish

Candlestick chart par price ki halchal ko samajhne ka tarika hai. Ek candle me ek 'Body' hoti hai (Open/Close range) aur do wicks (High/Low shadow) hoti hain. Green candle tezi (bullish) aur Red candle mandi (bearish) ko dikhati hai.

Asan Bhasha Me: Agar kisi candle ki neeche ki dandi bohot lambi hai, iska matlab hai buyers ne market girne se rokh kar wapas tezi se upar kheench liya hai.

AI Trend Indicators ek hi waqt par Moving Averages, RSI, aur Volume indicators ka calculations karte hain. Ye real-time data ko analyze karke bina kisi darr ya lalach ke perfect trend detect karte hain.

Call (CE) & Put (PE) Options

➕
🇬🇧 English

Options are derivatives contracts. A Call Option (CE - Call European) gives the buyer the right to buy the asset, usually bought when expecting the market to go UP. A Put Option (PE - Put European) gives the right to sell, bought when expecting the market to go DOWN.

Example: If Nifty is at ₹22,000 and you expect it to rise to ₹22,200, you buy CE. If you expect Nifty to drop, you buy PE.
🇮🇳 Hinglish

Options trading ke do sabse main types hote hain. Call Option (CE) tab khareeda jata hai jab aapko lagta hai market UPAR (tezi me) jayega. Put Option (PE) tab khareeda jata hai jab aapko lagta hai market NEECHE (mandi me) jayega.

Asan Bhasha Me: Agar lagta hai Nifty ₹22,000 se ₹22,200 bhagega, to CE khareedo. Agar lagta hai market crash ho kar niche girega, to PE khareedo!
📊

Put Call Ratio (PCR)

➕
🇬🇧 English

Put Call Ratio (PCR) is calculated by dividing the total open interest of Put options by the total open interest of Call options. PCR > 1 indicates Bullish sentiment (strong support), while PCR < 0.7 indicates Bearish sentiment (strong resistance).

Example: A PCR of 1.4 means option sellers are aggressively writing Puts, signaling a very strong market floor.
🇮🇳 Hinglish

PCR (Put Call Ratio) ka calculation total Puts ke OI ko total Calls ke OI se divide karke hota hai. Agar PCR 1.2 se bada hai, to market me tezi (Bullish) hai. Agar PCR 0.7 se chota hai, to market me mandi (Bearish) hai.

Asan Bhasha Me: PCR 1.4 ka matlab hai ki operators bohot saare Puts sell kar rahe hain, jo market me ek majboot support banata hai aur market upar bhaag sakta hai.

Open Interest (OI)

➕
🇬🇧 English

Open Interest (OI) represents the total number of active, outstanding derivative contracts. High OI strikes act as strong barriers (Support/Resistance) because that's where big institutional writers have deployed massive capital.

Example: If the ₹22,000 Strike Price has a Put Open Interest of 1 crore shares, it indicates operators are confident that Nifty will not expire below ₹22,000, making it unbreakable support.
🇮🇳 Hinglish

Open Interest (OI) ka matlab hai wo total contracts jo market me active hain. Option chain me jis strike price par sabse zyada OI hota hai, wo bohot bada Support ya Resistance ban jata hai kyunki wahan big operators ka paisa laga hota hai.

Asan Bhasha Me: Agar ₹22,000 level par Put OI 1 Crore shares hai, to operators ko poora bharosa hai ki market isse neeche nahi tootega, isliye ye level ek deewar ban jata hai!
